History of Salsa Dancing

Cuba is usually credited with the origin of salsa dancing, though the dance style carries influences from around the world (Central Home). Salsa is a mix of Haitian and French Danzón, African instrumentals, African slave Rumba, Cuban Són, and traditional Spanish music (Dance Facts).
