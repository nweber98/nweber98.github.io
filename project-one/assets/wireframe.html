Open Sans Condensed: a humanist sans serif

Type Director of Ascender Corp Steve Matteson designed Open Sans in 2011 after it was commissioned by Google. The font is a humanist sans serif typeface, according to Google Fonts. It is an excellent choice for digital platforms, according to Creative Bloq, because of its easy-to-read appearance in a variety of sizes. I chose the font because it is very simple, crisp and clean.
